Onigiri

AKA Rice balls AKA Burn your hands in exchange for food

The classic anime food for when going on a trip to the mountain

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Nori seaweed
  • Fillings can include:
    • Salmon
    • Ume (Japanese plum)
    • Bonito flakes with soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice as you would cook sushi rice (look up recipe if don't know, this is not a kindergarden, you useless fool.
  2. Put rice in separate container
  3. Fluff with wooden paddle and wait until it's not as hot as hell itself. Rice should not be cold, but getting your hands burned is also inconvenient.
  4. In the meantime you can prepare the fillings if they are not ready.
  5. Put water and salt in little bowls for easy grabbing.
  6. Wet hands with water from bowl, salt hands with salt from bowl. Not too much, not too little. How do you know it's too much? You don't.
  7. Gently grab rice, gently form a ball, gently press a hole in the middle.
  8. Gently add filling, gently try to close. Gently add more rice if necessary to close hole.
  9. Key word: Gently. Very important.
  10. Form triangle. Don't press hard on the rice, it cries sometimes.
  11. Cut the nori if you have full sheets.
  12. Wrap the nori around, this is for not getting your hands full of rice when eating.
  13. Done.
