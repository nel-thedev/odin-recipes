Spaghetti

Pasta Puttanesca to be precise

Traditionally made by prostitutes because it's cheap. Seems legit.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Canned cut tomato
  • Olive oil
  • Olives (Pitted if you don't hate yourself
  • Anchovies
  • Garlic
  • Parsley (Please for the love of God use fresh parsley, not flakes)

Steps:

  1. Pot of water on stove. Don't boil yet.
  2. Chop garlic. Make it fine or put it through a grater.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Medium heat.
  4. Chop olives.
  5. Anchovies in pan. Brown? Break them down.
  6. Garlic in pan. Brown? Add tomatoes.
  7. Squash tomato with paddle. Gets saucy.
  8. Olives in pan. Parsley in pan. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. You can add a bay leaf for flavor.
  10. Taste and adjust. Always test. If you're not full when you're done cooking you didn't taste enough.
  11. Boil water while sauce gets ready.
  12. Add salt to water. Throw pasta in.
  13. Al dente is the only acceptable way to cook pasta. It's not that hard, look it up.
  14. Drain pasta.
  15. Serve with parmesan cheese on top and some optional basil leaves.
