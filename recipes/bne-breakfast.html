B&E Breakfast

Classic Bacon and Eggs

Who doesn't like Bacon and Eggs for a quick breakfast?

Ingredients:

  • Some eggs
  • Some bacon
  • Optional toast, vegetables, whatever the heart desires

Steps:

  1. Bacon in.
  2. More bacon in, there is no such thing as too much bacon.
  3. Bacon crisp.
  4. Eggs in.
  5. Eggs cooked to taste. Yellow soft? Good. Yellow runny? Perfection.
  6. Season: Salt, pepper.
  7. Done.
